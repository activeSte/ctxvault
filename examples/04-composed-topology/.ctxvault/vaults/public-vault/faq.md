# Product FAQ

## What is the refund policy?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all plans. To request a refund, contact support with your order number. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

## How do I reset my password?
Go to the login page and click "Forgot password". Enter your email address and you will receive a reset link within 2 minutes. The link expires after 24 hours.

## What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal. All transactions are processed securely via Stripe.

## How do I upgrade my plan?
Log into your account, navigate to Settings > Billing, and select the plan you want to upgrade to. The change takes effect immediately and you are billed pro-rata for the remainder of the billing cycle.

## Can I export my data?
Yes. Go to Settings > Data > Export. You can export all your data in CSV or JSON format. Exports are available for download for 48 hours.

## What are your support hours?
L1 support is available 24/7 via chat. L2 technical support is available Monday to Friday, 9am to 6pm CET. L3 engineering support is available on-call for critical incidents.
